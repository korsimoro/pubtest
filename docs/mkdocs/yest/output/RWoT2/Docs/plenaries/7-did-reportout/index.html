Overall Model

  • Issuer might make claims about claims: this is a witness
  • Instead of Relying Party (geeky term of art): consumer, receiver
  • Oracle instead of public ledger; may not be static

Privacy

Identity is not disclosed, claims are - How do you protect claims from being sent on?

Choose what to disclose But then other people get to decide whether to trust you

Systems where you have to disclose (e.g., DMV) are different systems. Don't try to solve those! - The Holder has absolute control!

DID

  • Not Identity!
  • Not Verifiable Claims!

Discoverable, uniform, random - The value of a DID on its own is very small (when issued)

DID Object - Have Access Control to Update it - Point to Services that can do everything else - Must be able to do proof of ownership - Self-verifiable, self-validating
