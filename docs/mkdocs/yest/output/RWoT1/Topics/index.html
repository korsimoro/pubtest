In advance of the Rebooting the Web-of-Trust DesignShop on November 3rd & 4th, all participants were requested to submit a 1 or 2 page topics paper to be shared with other attendees on either: * A specific problem that you'd like to solve with a web-of-trust solution, and why current solutions (pgp or ca-based pki) can't address the problem? * A specific solution related to the web-of-trust that you'd like others to use or contribute to?
